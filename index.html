Ransom Help

If you are like me and enjoy a nice sweet treat, but you are too broke to afford candy, fear not! This is for you.

There are many ways to get free candy, but most of them either end in food poisining and prison. This is a very easy tutorial to follo, as it requires almost no skill whatsoever.

Follow these 5 easy steps to enjoy your candy!

  1. Walk down the street until you find a white van. It is even better if you can find one that says "FREE CANDY" as you can skip the next few steps.
  2. If it doesn't say "FREE CANDY", then you are going to have to walk up to the van and pace around until the person notices you.
  3. Once they notice you, one of two thing will happen. 1, they will tell you to go away as they are out of candy, or 2, they ask you if you want some candy.
  4. If they ask you if you want candy, then immediately say yes. They will ask you to get in the back of their van and if there is candy, CONGRATULATIONS! You got your free candy! If there is no candy, then there should be candy in the place you're going to.
  5. When you get to your destination, there should be candy! It has happened to everyone I've asked so far. If not, you will be tied up and held for ransom, and that means you failed. Go to the link above titled "Ransom Help" to find another tutorial on how to survive.
